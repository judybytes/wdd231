Site Plan: Artisan's Haven

Site Name

"Artisan's Haven" was chosen as the site name because it conveys both the nurturing community aspect ("Haven") and the focus on skilled craftspeople ("Artisan's"). The name suggests a welcoming space where artisans can showcase their work and connect with appreciative customers.

Potential domain: artisans-haven.com

Site Purpose

Artisan's Haven serves as a digital marketplace and community platform that:

  • Provides local artisans with a professional platform to showcase and sell their handcrafted items
  • Connects craft enthusiasts with authentic, handmade products
  • Facilitates knowledge sharing through workshops and community events
  • Promotes sustainable shopping through support of local craftspeople

Target Audience Scenarios

  • How can I find unique, handmade gifts created by local artisans in my area?
  • Where can I learn traditional crafting techniques from experienced artisans?
  • As an artisan, how can I reach more customers and grow my handcraft business?
  • What upcoming craft workshops or events are available in my community?
